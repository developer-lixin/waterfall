cities

Residents of cities are responsible for 80% of global greenhouse gas emissions. City greenhouse gas emissions reflect the structure of a city, its energy sources, and its residents lifestyles. Resource use, wastewater production, toxic releases, and solid waste generation are all linked among themselves and with greenhouse gas emissions as well. Cities have the opportunity to alter course, implement smart policies, and develop sustainable communities. Well managed, well designed, dense cities are also the only way to reduce the global warming effect.

fossil fuels

In the age of industrialization our reliance on artificial products that make us more comfortable is getting stronger day by day. Everything we are using needs power to work and we try to provide this power mostly by burning fossil fuels and other natural resources, As a result we produce more greenhouse gases like CO2, These gases in the atmosphere have trapped more heat on our planet which causes thermal expansion and distorts our lives as well as the lives of many plants and animals.

close

sea level

As a result of thermal expansion the land ice in our planet shrinks gradually and releases water into the oceans, Causing sea level to rise, On the other hand the ocean temperature increases and accelerates this process. While sea level rises, More frequent flooding happens, Hence saltwater intrudes into groundwater drinking supplies, contaminates irrigation supplies or overruns agricultural fields in the coastal communities where approximately 40% of the world's population live.

clean energy

Approximately 80% of global electricity produced today comes from non-renewable types of energy like fossil fuels which will be hard to find one day. Wind energy is one of the most popular and sensible types of renewable energies which is available freely in nature and exists infinitely, On the other hand converting these types of energies into electricity produces no heat-trapping gases, This is why some people also call it clean energy.

green planet

As less forests cover our planet, Less CO2 and other types of greenhouse gases is absorbed by plants and trees during photosynthesis, This accelerates the earth overheating process which is also called GLOBAL WARMING, So lets try saving our planet, Even planting a small tree could help to reduce the harm we have already caused and secure the future of humanity.
